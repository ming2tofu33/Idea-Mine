# Project Overview v2

> Internal alignment document for IDEA MINE v2. This document defines the product direction after the shift to a web-first product and a premium space UI, and is intended to be decomposed into `mind` later.

## 1. Project Summary

IDEA MINE v2 repositions the product from a mobile-first pixel experiment into a web-first premium idea exploration platform. The goal is not to change the core value of the product, but to express that value through a channel and an interface that better fit how the product will actually be used.

V2 is driven by two equally important shifts. The first is strategic: core product work moves from app-first to web-first. The second is experiential: the interface moves from pixel game aesthetics to a premium space UI built around depth, clarity, and smooth motion. Together, these shifts aim to improve validation speed, day-to-day usability, and overall brand quality.

## 2. Why V2

V1 proved that the mining metaphor, structured idea generation, and Mine-Lab-Vault loop have real product potential. It also revealed the limits of a mobile-first, pixel-heavy approach once the product started moving closer to a serious planning tool than a playful experiment.

IDEA MINE is fundamentally about reading, comparing, selecting, refining, and revisiting ideas. That usage pattern benefits more from the browser than from a small mobile screen. At the same time, the product now needs a stronger sense of quality and trust than the current pixel tone naturally provides.

V2 exists to answer that gap. It keeps the core product thesis intact while upgrading the primary channel, the design system, and the emotional tone to match the product IDEA MINE is becoming.

## 3. The Two Core Shifts

### 3.1 From App-First to Web-First

In v2, IDEA MINE is designed, built, and validated on the web first. The browser becomes the reference environment for the core user loop, and the app becomes a later extension built on top of a clearer product baseline.

This change reflects the real shape of the work. Users need room to compare results, read longer text, understand structure, and move between stored assets. The web supports those behaviors more naturally at this stage than a mobile-first product does.

### 3.2 From Pixel Game UI to Premium Space UI

In v2, IDEA MINE moves away from a pixel game presentation and toward a premium space interface. The product should feel like a high-quality exploration system rather than a retro-styled game.

This does not mean full 3D or spectacle-driven UI. The target is a 2.5D cinematic product interface: deep backgrounds, restrained lighting, smooth motion, readable panels, and selective graphic enhancement where it adds atmosphere without reducing usability.

### 3.3 Why These Shifts Belong Together

These two shifts reinforce each other. A web-first product still presented through a playful pixel UI risks feeling misaligned with its use case. A premium visual direction without a web-first product strategy risks becoming surface-level polish on top of the wrong foundation.

V2 treats channel and experience as one decision. The product must become more usable and more credible at the same time.

## 4. Product Vision

IDEA MINE v2 is a web-first product for exploring, combining, refining, and compounding idea assets with AI. It is not just a system for generating concepts on demand. It is a structured environment for moving from possibility to judgment to stored value.

The product should help users discover promising combinations, recognize which outputs deserve attention, and turn selected outputs into reusable planning assets. Generation is only the starting point. The real value of IDEA MINE lies in the full path from exploration to selection to refinement to accumulation.

In this model, the user is not a passive consumer of AI responses. The user is an explorer, selector, and builder. IDEA MINE should therefore feel closer to an observatory and research system than to a chat box.

The product is especially intended for people who need to turn vague possibility into structured direction, such as founders, planners, product thinkers, and researchers. V2 should make these users feel that they are using a serious creative instrument, not a novelty generator.

## 5. Core Metaphor

The core metaphor of IDEA MINE v2 is a premium exploration system built around signals, resources, and stored discoveries. Ideas are not treated as disposable text outputs. They are treated as signals to investigate, materials to refine, and assets to keep.

V2 keeps the mining structure but expands it into a broader spatial language. Instead of a pixel mine, the product moves toward imagery such as nebula fields, observatories, analysis stations, and archives. This preserves the meaning of the IDEA MINE name while making the metaphor feel more natural in a web product context.

The metaphor must support product structure, not distract from it. The Mine remains the place for discovery, the Vault remains the place for storage, and the Lab remains the place for development. The world-building should strengthen clarity, not compete with it.

## 6. Product Principles

1. Web-native productivity comes first. The browser is the primary environment for reading, comparing, organizing, and revisiting ideas.
2. Discovery matters more than raw generation volume. The product should help users find meaningful directions, not just produce more text.
3. Atmosphere must support clarity. Backgrounds, effects, and motion exist to enhance focus, not to overpower content.
4. Premium comes before playful. The product can feel imaginative and immersive, but it should first feel capable, serious, and well-made.
5. Every strong result should be able to become an asset. Good outputs must be saveable, revisitable, and expandable.
6. Structure comes before complexity. Navigation, hierarchy, and user flow must be clear before visual or feature richness expands.
7. The product must stay extensible. Current decisions should make later expansion to app, Showcase, Observatory, and other spaces easier rather than harder.

## 7. Web-First Strategy

The web is the base surface of v2. Core functionality should be designed, tested, and validated there before being translated into other channels. This changes both product planning and implementation planning.

The operational implication is simple. Mine, Vault, and Lab must work as a strong browser experience first. Information density, layout decisions, reading comfort, and workflow transitions should all be judged by web standards rather than by mobile constraints.

This strategy does not reject the app. It changes the order of development. The app should come later, after the core structure has already been proven on the web. At that point, the app can be designed for mobile-native use cases such as quick exploration, lightweight saving, notifications, and fast re-entry instead of being forced to carry the entire product from day one.

At the architecture level, web-first also means separating reusable logic from channel-specific UI. Data models, API contracts, and domain behavior should remain portable. The interface layer should be free to differ between web and app if that leads to a better experience on each surface.

## 8. Experience Direction

The experience of IDEA MINE v2 should combine exploration, analysis, and accumulation. Users should feel that they are discovering something meaningful, evaluating it with precision, and storing it inside a system that grows more valuable over time.

The overall tone should be cinematic but calm. Entering the product should feel distinctive and high-quality, but once the user starts working, the interface should become steady, legible, and unobtrusive. V2 should feel more like a premium exploration console than a flashy demo.

Motion is important, but it should be measured. Transitions, emphasis states, and interactions should be smooth, controlled, and intentional. Good motion in v2 should make the interface feel more refined and more trustworthy, not louder.

## 9. Brand and Visual Direction

The visual language of v2 moves away from retro pixel styling and toward a premium space identity. The goal is to create a product that feels futuristic, deep, and distinctive without becoming noisy or overdesigned.

The world of the product should draw from observatories, nebulae, signal systems, minerals, and archival technology. This world-building should influence the interface through background depth, panel treatment, light behavior, and visual tone rather than through heavy literal illustration.

At the material level, the interface should feel closer to glass, metal, radar panels, and refined terminal surfaces than to decorative game props. The visual system should imply precision equipment and high-value infrastructure rather than playful retro nostalgia.

Color should be restrained and deliberate. Deep dark foundations should support a small set of precise accent tones inspired by starlight, signal blue, soft cyan, faint violet, and controlled metallic highlights. The feeling should be premium and precise rather than neon-saturated.

Typography should separate brand expression from reading comfort. Display moments can carry a more futuristic identity, but body copy and working text must remain highly readable for longer sessions. The design system should create atmosphere in headers and structure, while preserving calm clarity in actual content.

Surfaces and components should feel engineered rather than playful. Cards, panels, buttons, and overlays should resemble refined instruments or analysis modules, not game props. The product can still be atmospheric, but it should communicate confidence and seriousness first.

## 10. Core User Loop

The core loop of IDEA MINE v2 is not just generation. It is discovery, comparison, selection, storage, and development.

1. In Mine, the user explores promising combinations and enters a discovery flow.
2. The user reviews generated results and decides which directions are worth keeping.
3. Strong results are saved to Vault as reusable idea assets.
4. Selected assets move into Lab, where they can be expanded into more structured planning material.

This loop must be repeatable and compounding. A saved asset should not become dead output. It should become material for future work and a signal for what the user wants to explore next.

## 11. Information Architecture

V2 should separate the public web layer from the working app layer. The public layer handles product introduction, entry, and future external-facing surfaces. The app layer is where the actual exploration and planning work happens.

Inside the app layer, Mine, Vault, and Lab form the primary structure. These spaces reflect the core loop and should be the clearest and strongest parts of the product.

Mine contains discovery and first-pass selection. Vault contains saved assets, browsing, re-entry, and organization. Lab contains refinement and development of selected assets into stronger planning outputs.

Other spaces such as Observatory, Showcase, and Exchange remain valid long-term directions, but they should be treated as later expansion layers rather than first-phase MVP priorities.

## 12. Tech Stack Direction

The technical direction of v2 should support a high-quality web experience without blocking later expansion.

- Frontend language: TypeScript
- Frontend framework: React + Next.js
- Motion: Framer Motion as the primary motion layer
- Graphics: CSS and Canvas by default, with React Three Fiber considered only for selective use
- Backend and data: keep the existing Supabase and Python backend structure where possible

TypeScript is the right base language because the product already depends on typed state, API responses, and reusable domain structures. React and Next.js provide the most natural path for building a web-first product with strong component structure, routing, and long-term maintainability.

The stack should stay product-first. More advanced graphics tools should only be introduced where they materially improve atmosphere or clarity without turning the product into a graphics project.

## 13. What We Keep

V2 keeps the core product thesis intact.

- Structured idea exploration rather than generic prompt-and-response behavior
- The Mine-Vault-Lab loop as the center of the product
- The larger mining and exploration metaphor
- The idea that generated outputs should become stored assets
- The existing Supabase and Python backend foundation where reusable
- The belief that IDEA MINE should feel like a distinctive product, not a generic productivity app

## 14. What We Change

V2 deliberately changes the following foundations.

- The primary platform shifts from app-first to web-first
- The emotional tone shifts from pixel game UI to premium space UI
- The product impression shifts from experimental and playful to refined and credible
- The interface shifts toward higher information density and stronger browser usability
- The design system shifts from pixel-native components to a new premium web UI system
- The roadmap shifts from feature breadth toward strengthening the core loop first

## 15. MVP Scope

The MVP for v2 should prove that the core browser experience is strong, useful, and coherent.

The minimum product should include:

- A clear web entry point and login flow
- A working Mine experience for exploration, generation, and first-pass selection
- A working Vault experience for storing and revisiting selected idea assets
- A working Lab experience for expanding saved ideas into more structured outputs
- A first version of the premium space design system with core layout, typography, card, button, and motion rules
- Real integration with the current backend and data layer for the main loop

The MVP is not just a clickable concept. It must be a real product slice that can become the base of future expansion.

## 16. Non-Goals

The following are intentionally not goals for v2 at this stage.

- Shipping a fully polished mobile app before the web product is validated
- Turning the interface into a full 3D or game-like experience
- Continuing the pixel style as the primary visual language
- Expanding feature count before the Mine-Vault-Lab loop is strong
- Framing the product as a simple AI idea generator without asset-building depth
- Forcing one identical UI across every platform

V2 is about making the product sharper, not broader.

## 17. Future Expansion

Once the web-first core is strong, IDEA MINE can expand in several directions.

- A mobile app focused on quick exploration, light saving, and re-entry
- Observatory as a data and pattern-viewing layer
- Showcase as an external-facing layer for selected outputs
- Exchange as a later layer for connection, opportunity, or asset circulation
- Richer asset structures such as collections, comparisons, and multi-step planning artifacts
- Deeper brand expression and more advanced motion or visual systems where appropriate

These expansions only make sense after the central web loop is proven and stable.
